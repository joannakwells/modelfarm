About

Where vineyard work comes first.

Model Farm is a small project focused on mountain-grown Cabernet, Chardonnay, Syrah, and rosé from coastal California. We work with heritage sites, native fermentations, and patient élevage to make wines that feel both grounded and alive.

We came to wine through curiosity, night shifts, and a belief that farming still matters. The work starts in old vines on steep hillsides, following the rhythm of each season rather than a fixed recipe.

In the cellar, we keep things simple: native fermentations, gentle handling, long lees contact, and time. The goal is not to style the wines into something fashionable, but to give each site enough space to speak for itself.

Model Farm exists for people who care where things come from and how they are made. If that is you, you are already part of the story.
